# Nour — Islamic Audio Library

## App name
Nour

## Short description (≤ 80 characters)
Listen to the Quran, lectures, and adhkar in Arabic and English, anytime.

## Full description
Nour is your personal Islamic audio library. Stream and download the Quran with multiple reciters, explore lectures by scholars, and access morning and evening adhkar (remembrances).

### Features
- **Quran**: Multiple reciters, verse-by-verse playback, bookmarks, tafsir
- **Lectures**: Curated playlists by category
- **Adhkar**: Guided morning and evening remembrances
- **Offline**: Download tracks to listen without an internet connection
- **Prayer Times**: Location-based prayer time notifications with Azan
- **Dark & Light themes**: Fully bilingual (Arabic & English), RTL support

### Permissions
- **Location** (optional): To show accurate prayer times for your city
- **Notifications** (optional): For Azan (call to prayer) and adhkar reminders
- **Audio** (required): To play audio content

### Privacy
This app does not sell or share your personal data. See our privacy policy for details.
